Saturday Night

Join us for an evening of great company, drinks, and music.

The Menu

A curated selection of bites and refreshments.

Appetizers

Artisanal cheese board, truffle popcorn, and seasonal bruschetta.

Main Fare

Wood-fired pizzas and gourmet sliders (Vegetarian options available).

Drinks

Craft cocktails, local wines, and a selection of Japanese whiskies.

Getting Here

Rideshare

We highly recommend using Uber or Lyft. Drop-off is right at the front entrance.

Parking

Street parking is available but limited. There is a public garage two blocks North on Emerson St.

Public Transit

We are a 10-minute walk from the local Caltrain station.

FAQs

What is the dress code?

Smart casual. Wear whatever makes you feel ready to celebrate!

Can I bring a plus one?

Please check your specific invitation or reach out to us directly so we can manage the headcount.

Are pets allowed?

While we love animals, we ask that you leave your furry friends at home for this evening.

Should I bring anything?

Your presence is enough! However, if you have a specific bottle of wine you'd love to share, feel free.
